AI Agents

AI Agents grounded in your data.
Embedded in your workflow.

Our agents are not chatbots in a sidebar or AI bolted onto a control tower. They run on your data, plug into your workflow, and back every output with evidence. The result is AI your team actually trusts and acts on.

01

Grounded in your data.

Agents query your uploaded datasets: part catalog, plant schedules, supplier scores, contract terms, operational history. Not a generic logistics corpus. Not a public model guessing at your business.

02

Embedded in your workflow.

Agents are triggered by workflow events and return results back into the workflow. A vessel arrives at port and a customs prediction agent fires. A shipment slips behind schedule and a delay risk agent escalates. They run inside the process, not alongside it.

03

Evidence-backed.

Every output carries a reasoning chain and source citations. Outputs without citations are rejected before they reach you. Your team sees the conclusion, the data behind it, and a clear path to act.

This is what runs your operation, not what describes it. The plant does not stop. The P&L line does not take the hit. OTIF holds.
